Bubble Watch

Digital wristwatch using a HP bubble LED display and an Adafruit Feather M0. Includes a 3D-printed case that fits most watch bands.

Parts

  • Adafruit Feather M0 basic proto
  • 7 330Ohm resistors, 1/4 or less (1/8 could make it more compact)
  • HP QDSP-6064 4 digit seven segment LED display
  • 100mAh LiPo battery
  • replacement watch wristband with spring pins
